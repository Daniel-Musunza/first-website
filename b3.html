Lately I've been obsessed with women's t-shirtswhite with writing, which go well with everything, I like to combine them with slim black jeans, but which can also go well with classic trousers or with a beautiful longuette, I can already imagine a look: T-shirt, bell skirt and a beautiful jacket, in this case we can also opt for sneakers or decolétté depending on the situation in which the outfit is put ...
