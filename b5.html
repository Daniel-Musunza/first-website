Here is Crepetray TM the ultra light sneakers

Spring has now arrived, even if you don't feel very much because, I must admit, that this cold Milanese doesn't allow me to show off all my spring outfits that I have already ready in the wardrobe for a few days. I have a great passion for shoes, and I think I have found the perfect compromise between comfort, tradition and trend. Ecco is a Danish brand, an organized and visionary large family that focuses on innovation and on guaranteeing a result at the top of the possibilities. Behind every product there are huge mechanisms, people of extraordinary creativity who love their craft and are committed to producing fabulous creations.

Their greatest strength is the company's employees who work and collaborate with each other to churn out great products of excellent quality. For this spring / summer 2018, I have chosen the Ecco innovation and its new ultra light contemporary yet apparently very classic Ecco Crepetray TM sneaker. An innovative sole that ensures comfort at all times, wraps the foot and absorbs shocks while maintaining an essential grip to avoid any pain that could be caused by an uncomfortable shoe. Ideal for the foot, I chose the sneakers with laces precisely because I feel that my foot is protected in any situation, the Ecco CrepetrayTM Ladies are made with refined materials that give a super exclusive shape.

Comfortable, trendy, to refresh the look for this beautiful season ... I can't wait to be able to wear them on my working days but also during my holidays ... they are so comfortable that I could really use them anywhere, then they are also pink ... and me I'm crazy about pink !!!! The Danish brand has now entered my heart and will never come out from there because each collection has truly incredible peculiarities, trendy shoes, comfortable shoes, cool shoes that have something very traditional and elegant but wrapped in great innovation and technicality. The ecco.com site is structured in a very clear way, a fundamental feature in my opinion, precisely because the sites must be like a shop window, well organized, with easily available products. Each pair of shoes and each collection tells a story, a meeting between the past and the present, very reassuring for a woman like me who can never settle for anything. With the Ecco CrepetrayTM Ladies pink I can start this sunny period with comfort and a lot of freshness!
